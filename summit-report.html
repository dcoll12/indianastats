Mission
The Indiana Rural Summit is building a statewide coalition of candidates, volunteers, and organizers committed to contesting every corner of Indiana in 2026. By combining real precinct-level election data with purpose-built organizing tools, the Summit equips rural campaigns with the data intelligence, coordination infrastructure, and community networks they need to compete — from CD-8 to the smallest House district.
Platform & Tools Built
🗺️
IN-Index Dashboard
Interactive partisan lean index for all 159 Indiana legislative districts. Sortable tables with 2020–2024 margins, vote counts, and competitiveness scores updated through the 2024 cycle.
Static Web App
🌻
Rural Summit Directory
Searchable, filterable contact directory for Summit participants — candidates, volunteers, organizers. Backed by Google Sheets with live photo cards, role filters, district/county search, and auto-sync forms.
Streamlit · Google Sheets
🕸️
Power Packs Network
Visual candidate network showing all 2026 races as an interactive graph. Nodes sized by competitiveness, colored by party. Highlights Rural Summit cohort members and shows district interconnections.
SVG · D3-style Viz
📍
District Match Tool
Instant county-to-district and district-to-county lookup covering all 92 Indiana counties. Search any county to see its House, Senate, and Congressional districts — no backend required.
Browser-Based · 92 Counties
