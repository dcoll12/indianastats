About the Summit
The Indiana Rural Summit recruits and supports Democrats running in districts that have gone too long without a serious challenger. The 2026 cohort of 56 candidates contests 49 unique seats — 31 House, 15 Senate, 2 Congressional (CD-5 & CD-9), and 1 statewide SOS race — including 3 seats where cohort members face each other in a primary. Statewide, Democrats are contesting 124 of Indiana's 159 legislative seats, the broadest field in years. The Summit gives cohort members the data, connections, and infrastructure rural campaigns rarely get.
