About Tandem Fundraisers

The Indiana Rural Summit organizes across county and district lines — especially in areas shaped by "artificially rural" districts — to maximize shared resources and collective impact. A Power Pack is a small group of current political candidates in a close geographic area who join to support each other, creating efficient campaigns and motivating voters to vote up and down the ballot.

Donate links go directly to ActBlue pages managed by the Indiana Rural Summit. Questions? Contact the campaign organizers through the Power Packs page.
